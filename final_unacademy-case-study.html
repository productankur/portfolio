The Brief

Assignment from Unacademy

The ask

Design a data schema for a content management system — covering grades, boards, subjects, chapters, concepts, learning outcomes, lesson plans, and questions — with full student tracking.

Scope

01 — What I Built

Three systems, one shared data model

LMS, CMS, and Assessments were designed as interconnected layers over a shared content graph. Learning outcomes sit at the centre — linking every question, concept, and class to a trackable student goal.

System 01

LMS

Class and user management with roles for students, teachers, and admins. Classes map to boards, grades, and subjects with lesson plans.

Learning Management
System 02

CMS

A content hierarchy: Subject → Concepts → Chapters → Topics → Sub-topics. Each node supports videos, PDFs, and live sessions.

Content Management
System 03

Assessments

A question bank and quiz engine with MCQs, subjective answers, assignments, and gamification — each mapped to learning outcomes.

02 — Metrics

Measuring real learning, not just traffic

North star metrics chosen to track genuine engagement. The DAU/MAU ratio is the key health signal — it shows whether students are building a daily learning habit.

📅 DAU Daily active learners
📆 MAU Monthly retention
📊 WAU Weekly habit formation
⏱️ Session Time Depth per visit
🎬 Content/User Video duration per learner
